I'm Jeremy King. I live in central Florida, where I work as an electrical engineer designing industrial computers and co-own a cigar shop. I'm moving to Rome.

I build things: hardware, infrastructure, businesses. My current projects include recreating the Intel 4004 microprocessor, designing an open-source baseboard management controller, and running a self-hosted server stack that handles everything from music to document management.

I believe in the small web. This site has no JavaScript, no tracking, no analytics, no cookies, and no ads. It loads in under 50KB. It's built with Hugo and hosted on Cloudflare Pages.
