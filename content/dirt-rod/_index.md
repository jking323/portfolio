title: "Dirt Rod"
---

A build log for the dirt rod — part Jeep, part rat rod, all mongrel. The plan
is simple to say and hard to do: take an old Jeep, strip it to the bones, and
put it back together as something lower, meaner, and unapologetically rough
around the edges. Patina stays. Chrome is optional. It has to run dirt.
